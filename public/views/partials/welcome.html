Thank you to all the voters of district 4!

Hi, my name is Jono! I am an urban planner, proud South High graduate, and a product of our parks. I do community-based engagement, planning, and research work for a wide array of public, private, and non-profit organizations in the Twin Cities.

  • Parks for and by the people. Let’s empower local stewardship of our parks, by allowing more local, active participation in bettering our parks, moving towards participatory budgeting, and creating meaningful partnerships with community groups.
  • Safe parks are active parks. We should be spending more money on youth programming than we do on police enforcement, not the other way around.
  • Be bold stewards of our environment to combat climate change. Let’s invest in rain gardens, carbon sequestration, and end the use of pesticides in the parks.
  • Weave an equity framework through all park decisions. I am well-versed in performing equity and policy analyses. I wrote a transformative equity-based study on capital budget spending in Saint Paul that spurred an overhaul of the entire spending process. I will bring this perspective to evaluating all our park spending.
